What drives me

Passionate about complex problem solving in accounting, where the right answer comes from combining what the codification permits with the real-world business strategy behind the numbers.

The work I find most rewarding sits at the intersection of technical accounting and business context. Getting the ASC citation right is table stakes. The real value is understanding why a company structured a deal a certain way, what the business implications are, and how the accounting treatment should reflect that reality. I lean heavily on AI to handle the volume and the repetitive layers of the work so that my time goes toward what actually requires judgment, experience, and a seat at the table.
